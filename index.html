What Is a TI-84 Graphing Calculator?

The TI-84 graphing calculator is a scientific and graphing device designed to perform complex mathematical operations. Unlike standard calculators, it can visualize equations, analyze datasets, solve matrices, and evaluate advanced mathematical functions.

The online version replicates many of the capabilities of the physical device while offering the convenience of browser-based access.

Graphing

Visualize linear, quadratic, polynomial, and trigonometric functions.

Scientific Operations

Perform advanced calculations involving logarithms, exponents, radicals, and trigonometric functions.

Statistics

Analyze datasets, perform regressions, and calculate descriptive statistics.

Matrices

Solve systems of equations and perform matrix operations efficiently.

How to Perform Basic Calculations

Beginners should start by practicing simple arithmetic operations.

  1. Open the calculator.
  2. Enter your mathematical expression.
  3. Press the ENTER key.
  4. Review the displayed result.
Examples:
  • 25 + 75
  • 15 × 18
  • √225
  • sin(45)
  • log(1000)

Repeated practice with these operations builds familiarity and confidence.

Graphing Equations Step by Step

Graphing capabilities distinguish TI-84 calculators from ordinary calculators.

  1. Select the Y= menu.
  2. Enter an equation such as:
    • Y = X² + 4X + 2
  3. Press GRAPH.
  4. Observe the plotted graph.
  5. Use TRACE to inspect specific points.

Graph visualization helps students understand mathematical relationships, identify intercepts, and analyze function behavior.
